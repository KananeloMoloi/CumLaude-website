About Us

Cumlaude Research Institute is a 100% black owned company. Although the company is situated in Johannesburg, the company is operational in 3 regions in the country namely Johannesburg, free State and Eastern Cape. Cumlaude Institute is also Quality Management System Design and Implementation Institute, whereby we do research and furthermore we are QCTO accredited training provider which operates within the boundaries of the Republic of SA and abroad even though we are based in Free State. We offer Internship programmmes to graduates and Learneship programmes to the Youth of South Africa including people living with disabilities.

Vision

Our goal is achieved through amongst others, disability mainstreaming and inclusion as well as advocacy for recognition of the disabled and abled thus ensuring their inclusion in all spheres of employement/skills programmes that seek to enhance the wellbeing of the society at large.
