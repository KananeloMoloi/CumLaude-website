Skills Programmes

Skills programmes are concise yet highly practical training courses that have been accredited by the Sector Education and Training Authority (SETA). These programmes are specifically designed to address targeted skill gaps and developmental needs within a particular company or industry, ensuring that employees acquire the necessary expertise to enhance productivity, efficiency, and overall workplace performance.

Work Integrated Learning

Work Integrated Learning (WIL) refers to structured educational programs that establish a direct connection between university students and workplaces relevant to their field of study. These programs are designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical, real-world experience by providing students with opportunities to apply their theoretical knowledge in professional settings.
