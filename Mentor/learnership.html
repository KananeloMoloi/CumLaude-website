Learnerships

We develop our learners with a good balance of structured learning and host employment for practical training and skills development

Cumlaude Institute offers accredited learnerships and accredited skills programmes. Our learnership programmes run for a 12 month period, which consist of 70% workplace experience in the actual field of study and 30% theory in the classroom.
