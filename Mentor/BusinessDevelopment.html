Mining Qualifications Authority Project 2024

This project aims to support Small Medium and Macro Enterprises with relevant training and financial support that are 100% owned by South african citizens. Priority is given to businesses owned by women, youth and people living with disability. Companies participating in this programme are compliant with South African Revenue Services and Unemployment Insurance Fund

MQA Project comprise of the following:

  • Apply innovative thinking to the development of a small business
  • Apply the principles of costing and pricing to a business venture
  • Demonstrate an understanding of an entrepreneurial profile
  • Demonstrate an understanding of the function of the market mechanisms in a new venture
  • Explain and apply the concept, principles and theories of motivation in a leadership context
  • Implement an action plan for a new venture
  • Plan and manange production/operations in a new venture
  • Plan strategically to improve new venture performance
  • Produce business plans for new ventures
  • Manage finances of a new venture
